About me

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology at the State Key Laboratory of Networking and Switching Technology, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), under the guidence of Professor Bo Cheng and Professor Junliang Chen. Before that, I received the B.S. degree from Jilin University (JLU), Changchun, China, in 2017. My research interests include network function virtualization, software defined network, and cloud computing.
